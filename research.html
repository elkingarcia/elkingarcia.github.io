Computer Architecture and Parallel Systems Laboratory (CAPSL)
Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering
University of Delaware
Newark, DE, U.S.A.

I work in the area of Parallel Computing. I am interested in solving new challenges for increasing performance and energy efficiency on present and future many-core architectures. Particularly, I work in solutions that involve new algorithms, compiler optimizations or new runtime system features. I am also interested in fine-grain energy-aware data-driven execution models as a possible solution to some of these challenges.
